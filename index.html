Our very first project, A subtly strange adventure game
While returning home, Leonard lost control of his car and ended up in a ditch. He regains consciousness in the room of a small inn, in the middle of an isolated country village. While his memory fails him, he will meet a series of strange characters who will each in their own way allow him to reflect on the meaning of his life.
Are you interested in this story? We tell you about our ongoing development and its twists and turns on our social networks.
